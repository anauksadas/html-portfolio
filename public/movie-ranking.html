My top 3 movies of all-time

The murder investigation into a young man's death takes an unexpected turn when the cops discover that the prime suspect has a lookalike.

Knives Out

Knives Out is a 2019 American mystery film written and directed by Rian Johnson, who also serves as a co-producer. It follows master detective Benoit Blanc investigating the death of the patriarch of a wealthy, dysfunctional family.

Persuasion

Persuasion is a 2022 American romantic period drama film based on Jane Austen's 1817 novel of the same name.
